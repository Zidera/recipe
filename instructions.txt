Really easy Brigadeiro Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 1 can of sweetened condensed milk (395g)
- 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- Chocolate sprinkles (for decoration)

Instructions:
1. In a medium saucepan, mix the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
2. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly, to avoid sticking.
3. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the pan, about 10-15 minutes.
4. Let it cool slightly, then grease your hands with butter and roll the mixture into small balls.
5. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles to coat them.
6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a firmer texture.

Enjoy your homemade brigadeiros!
